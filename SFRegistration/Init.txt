To use these classes, you have to follow some of the steps
1. Create a Custom Object : SFRegistration
	Fields :
		Company : Text (100)
		Email : Email (Select all check boxes to make it unique)
		Lead : LookUp(Lead)
		Account : LookUp(Account)
		Contact : LookUp(Contact)
